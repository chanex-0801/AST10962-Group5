High Street Haunted House

- Crying and singing voices: Residents claimed to hear eerie sounds at night.
- Ghostly apparitions: Tales of headless figures and shadows drifting along the corridors.
- Basement myths: The underground car park is rumored to have been a morgue, with one particular spot (“C44”) said to bring misfortune.
- Execution site: Stories suggest Japanese forces executed prisoners here, adding to its haunted reputation.
